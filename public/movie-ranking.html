Best Movies According to Aaron

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Edward Scissorhands

Beautiful music, a lot of metaphor and meaning can be derived from it. Manages to be moody but also funny.

Digimon The Movie

Look... it just hits hard, okay? Music is nostalgic, the concept is cool, time travel, mourning, healing, friendship, what more can you ask for?

The Matrix

So much great content. Lots to talk about post movie and appreciate when rewatched, really interesting ideas. It's a movie I'll always be down to watch.
